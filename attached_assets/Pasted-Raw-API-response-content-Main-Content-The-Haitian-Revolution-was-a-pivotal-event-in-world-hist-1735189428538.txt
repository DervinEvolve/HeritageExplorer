The Haitian Revolution was a pivotal event in world history that began in 1791 and ended in 1804. It was a successful slave revolt that led to the establishment of Haiti as the first independent black nation in the world. The revolution was marked by significant political, social, and economic changes, both within Haiti and in the broader Atlantic World.
